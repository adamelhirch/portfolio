Flori is known for his superb hemp products
that he sells on local markets around Zurich.
At the beginning of 2020, Corona hit Switzerland, and the country went into lockdown. For small businesses like Flori, this was a huge problem. He had no place to sell his Products anymore. So in March, he contacted me and asked me to make a eCommerce website for him. The challenge was to create a unique-looking site that represents the high quality of the products.
