NutriSight

Nutritional Analysis

Dashboard

NutriSight is a business intelligence project that transforms the Open Food Facts dataset into interactive Power BI dashboards. The project involved building a complete ETL pipeline with KNIME to clean, normalize, and enrich nutritional data from thousands of food products, then visualizing key insights around Nutri-scores, sales patterns, and product composition.

The Challenge

The Open Food Facts database contains millions of entries with inconsistent formatting, missing values, and unstructured nutritional data. The challenge was to build a reliable data pipeline that could clean and standardize this raw data, compute meaningful Nutri-scores, and present actionable insights through interactive dashboards — enabling users to explore products by category, country, and nutritional quality at a glance.

Technical

Stack

ETL: KNIME Analytics Platform — data cleaning, normalization, and transformation workflows
Visualization: Microsoft Power BI — interactive dashboards with slicers, gauges, and drill-down
Data Source: Open Food Facts (CSV/API) — global open-source food database
Analysis: Nutri-score computation, nutritional composition breakdown, sales pattern analysis
Data Processing: Excel, CSV manipulation, data quality validation

Key

Insights

Product Search Dashboard: Interactive product explorer with real-time Nutri-score gauge, energy metrics (kJ/100g), quantity sold, and detailed ingredient composition (fat, sugar, protein, fiber, salt) displayed as donut charts. Product locations are mapped geographically.

Sales Analysis Dashboard: Multi-dimensional breakdown of sales by food category (Groceries, Snacks, Confectioneries...), Nutri-score distribution across 2.1M+ entries, sales by country (US 42%, France 27%, UK 15%), and top-selling individual products.

Key

Achievements

• Processed and cleaned thousands of food products from the Open Food Facts database
• Built a complete KNIME ETL pipeline for data normalization and quality assurance
• Designed 2 interactive Power BI dashboards with drill-down capabilities and dynamic slicers
• Implemented Nutri-score computation and visualization with intuitive gauge charts
• Analyzed sales patterns across 5 countries and 11+ food categories
• Created a searchable product explorer with real-time nutritional breakdown
