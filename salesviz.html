Overview

SalesViz is an interactive PowerBI dashboard built to analyze and visualize the OpenFoodFacts database, one of the largest open-source food product databases in the world. The project transforms raw nutritional data into actionable insights through compelling visualizations.

The Challenge

OpenFoodFacts contains millions of food products with complex nutritional information, ingredient lists, quality scores, and categorical data. The challenge was to extract meaningful patterns and trends from this massive dataset and present them in an accessible, interactive format.

Data Processing & Modeling

Built a robust data model in PowerBI with optimized relationships and calculated measures using DAX. The data pipeline includes:

  • Data cleaning and transformation of raw OpenFoodFacts exports
  • Creation of dimension tables for products, categories, and brands
  • Calculation of nutritional KPIs and quality metrics
  • Time-based analysis for product evolution and trends

Key Visualizations

The dashboard features multiple interactive pages with:

  • Nutritional Analysis: Distribution of macronutrients, sugar content, salt levels across categories
  • Quality Scores: Nutri-Score and NOVA group distributions with comparative analysis
  • Category Deep-Dive: Detailed breakdowns by product category, brand, and country
  • Trend Analysis: Evolution of nutritional values and product launches over time
  • Comparative Views: Side-by-side comparison of similar products and brands

Interactive Features

Implemented advanced PowerBI features including:

  • Dynamic filtering across multiple dimensions
  • Drill-through capabilities for detailed product analysis
  • Bookmarks for saved views and story-telling
  • Custom tooltips with contextual information
  • Mobile-optimized layouts for on-the-go access

Impact & Insights

The dashboard enables users to:

  • Identify nutritional trends across food categories
  • Compare brand strategies and product positioning
  • Discover patterns in quality scores and certifications
  • Make data-driven decisions about food choices and market analysis
